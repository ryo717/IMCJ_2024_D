The public consultation revealed six main clusters of opinions on NTT Docomo. Participants expressed concerns about the company's contract terms, customer service, and network disruptions, while also discussing their experiences with the company's entertainment services. There was a mix of views on switching to Docomo, with some attracted by its perks and collaborations, but others wary of potential issues. Participants also discussed various promotions and referral codes for mobile services, shared experiences with NTT Docomo shops and collaborations, and compared different data plans and services.
